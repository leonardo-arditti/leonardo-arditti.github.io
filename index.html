Projects

EasyRide

Closed Source

EasyRide consists of a project developed during the second period of the last year of high school for the subject 'Project management and business organization'. The aim of the project was to develop an application that would allow (in a simulated way) to rent electric bikes in the Livorno area both through a desktop program with a GUI developed in Java and through a web interface with the use of PHP, JavaScript , HTML and CSS.

DeltaTrade

Closed Source

DeltaTrade is a project that was commissioned as a test for the state exam of the year 2019/2020. The goal was to produce a paper that would propose an overall solution to DeltaTrade's e-commerce problems: a company that wishes to develop its own IT platform to bring together demand and supply for the sale of various goods online. In addition to justifying the choices made, all systemic aspects were highlighted in the report (architectures, infrastructures, protocols and network services; components, devices, servers and related characteristics; ...) and IT (database, dynamic web interfaces), ...) necessary to support the activities and needs of both DeltaTrade and all the other entities of the scenario described.

GTW - Guess The Word

Open Source

First project used to test JavaScript functionality within a browser game. The game consists of guessing a random word obtained from the English dictionary within a predetermined number of attempts. The resulting site implements the game, is responsive and allows playability on any device.

Ciak si gira - a short film for Avis

The project "CIAK SI GIRA - A SHORT FILM FOR AVIS" had as its objective the realization of a short film that would lead the young people of Livorno to donate blood and other components. The project took place in the first year of "Informatics and telecommunications" and immediately presented itself as a good opportunity to collaborate with fellow students in order to produce a professional video. The group members were divided between actors and filming / post production staff, where I was present. The resulting short film was a great success during the presentation in the final phase, leading us to win the 3rd place of the 2017-2018 edition.
